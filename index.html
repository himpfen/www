Operating principles

Quiet structure. Clear ownership. Useful output.

Separate the audience from the engine.

Media properties can be public-facing while labs and infrastructure stay focused on systems.

Use brands only where they help.

Not every project needs a large identity. Some need a clean home and a clear purpose.

Build for maintenance.

The structure should make it easier to sustain projects, not just launch them.

Keep the umbrella simple.

Himpfen explains the ecosystem without forcing everything into one platform narrative.
