<div class="content-grid">
  <div class="content-card">
    <h2>Separate the audience from the engine.</h2>
    <p>Media brands, public research, tools, and infrastructure should not all be presented in the same way. The structure separates public-facing properties from the technical systems that support them.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="content-card">
    <h2>Build for maintenance.</h2>
    <p>Launching projects is easier than maintaining them. The ecosystem is organized around durability, clear ownership, repeatable workflows, and long-term stewardship.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="content-card">
    <h2>Use brands only where they help.</h2>
    <p>Some projects benefit from a standalone identity. Others are better treated as repositories, research outputs, or internal systems. The goal is clarity, not brand sprawl.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="content-card">
    <h2>Keep the umbrella simple.</h2>
    <p>Himpfen should explain the portfolio without becoming another platform narrative. The umbrella exists to organize the work, not to compete with it.</p>
  </div>
</div>
